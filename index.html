Business Plan
Skyside Coffee · Common Ground & High Ground · Phoenix, AZ
🎯
Summary

Skyside Coffee is a downtown coffee shop located on a corner in the heart of the city. The shop operates across two distinct floors — Common Ground (Floor 1) serves the fast-moving morning crowd with speed and efficiency, while High Ground (Floor 2) offers a scenic, elevated lounge experience for afternoons, sunsets, and evenings. Accessible via elevator and staircase, High Ground features floor-to-ceiling windows, ambient lighting, and an open-air balcony where guests can watch the horizon unfold in real time — enjoying an exclusive evening menu as the sky shifts from golden hour to nightfall. Together, the two floors serve everyone — commuters, remote workers, students, families, and downtown visitors — under one brand tagline: "Catch the horizon at Skyside."

🏆
Mission & Vision
  • Mission: To serve every customer an exceptional experience, whether they have 3 minutes or 3 hours
  • Vision: Become downtown's most iconic two-floor coffee destination
  • Values: Community, craft, atmosphere, and intentional hospitality
  • Floor 1 — Common Ground: "Your daily grind, perfected"
  • Floor 2 — High Ground: "Catch the horizon at Skyside"
👥
Target Market
  • Morning commuters (6–10am) — fast grab & go from Common Ground
  • Remote workers & students (10am–2pm) — settle into High Ground with a view
  • Lunch crowd (11am–1pm) — quick drinks + light bites on either floor
  • Afternoon walkers & shoppers (2–5pm) — impulse stop, iced drinks
  • Evening crowd (5–9pm) — sunset views and Golden Hour menu at High Ground
  • College students — ASU Downtown, Phoenix College, GCU & all AZ universities via 15% Student Grind discount
  • Office workers — Daily Grind Pass ($45/mo) for downtown professionals within walking distance
  • Estimated daily customers: 150–300 across both floors

  • Location: Phoenix, AZ — two-floor standalone building

  • Low sales: Offer daily specials & loyalty cards to drive repeat visits
